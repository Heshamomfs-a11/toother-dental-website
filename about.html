About Us

Your reliable dental supply partner in Australia

Our Story

Toother Dental Supplies was founded with a clear mission: to provide dental professionals across Australia with reliable, high-quality products at competitive prices. Based in Chifley, Canberra (ACT), we understand the unique needs of dental practices and strive to be a dependable partner in their success.

Our Mission

We are committed to supporting dental professionals by supplying the tools, materials, and equipment they need to deliver outstanding patient care. Every product we stock meets rigorous quality standards, and we stand behind everything we sell.

TGA Registration

Toother Dental Supplies is registered with the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA) as an authorised sponsor. This means we meet the regulatory requirements for the supply of therapeutic goods in Australia, giving you confidence in the products we provide.
